Typhoon Club

Year: 1985

Genre: drama film

Country: Japan

Plot: Typhoon Club is a monthly children's magazine published by Cyclone Publishing Ltd in Hong Kong. It was launched in 2002 and is designed to encourage local primary and secondary students to enjoy their reading of English. Much of its content is localised to Hong Kong and Asia, but it includes international content, too. Typhoon Club is used in many classrooms in Hong Kong as an alternative teaching tool. Over 180 issues have been published since its launch and there are over 1,200 features included within the magazine's back catalogue. Typhoon Club became an online magazine in October 2021.
