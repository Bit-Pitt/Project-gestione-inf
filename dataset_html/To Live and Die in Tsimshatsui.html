To Live and Die in Tsimshatsui

Year: 1994

Genre: crime film

Country: Hong Kong

Plot: To Live and Die in Tsimshatsui is a 1994 Hong Kong crime film directed by Andrew Lau and starring Jacky Cheung, Tony Leung, Jacklyn Wu and Power Chan. The film (sometimes referred to as New Edge Man) is said to be a remake of a film titled Man on the Brink.
