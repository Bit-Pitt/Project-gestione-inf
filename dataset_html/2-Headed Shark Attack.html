2-Headed Shark Attack

Year: 2012

Genre: adventure film

Country: United States

Plot: 2-Headed Shark Attack is a 2012 American independent thriller film with an ensemble cast by The Asylum, released on January 31, 2012 in the United States. Directed by Christopher Ray, the film stars Carmen Electra, Charlie O'Connell, Brooke Hogan, Joseph Velez, Christina Bach, David Gallegos and Corinne Nobili. The film premiered on September 8, 2012 on Syfy.
