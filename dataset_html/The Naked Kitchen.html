The Naked Kitchen

Year: 2009

Genre: romance film

Country: South Korea

Plot: The Naked Kitchen (Korean: 키친; RR: Kichin; lit. Kitchen) is a 2009 South Korean romantic comedy-drama film written and directed by Hong Ji-young. Starring Shin Min-ah, Ju Ji-hoon, and Kim Tae-woo, the film is a romantic comedy-drama about a very curious ménage à trois.
