Zur Hölle mit den Paukern

Year: 1968

Genre: comedy film

Country: Germany

Plot: Zur Hölle mit den Paukern is a 1968 West German comedy film directed by Werner Jacobs and starring Hans Kraus, Theo Lingen and Günther Schramm. It was the first entry into the seven part Die Lümmel von der ersten Bank series of comedy films.
