Séraphin: Heart of Stone

Year: 2002

Genre: drama film

Country: Canada

Plot: Séraphin: Heart of Stone (French: Séraphin: un homme et son péché, Lit. "Séraphin: A man and his sin") is a 2002 Quebec drama film. The script is based on the novel Un homme et son péché by Claude-Henri Grignon. The film won six Prix Jutra for Best Actor (Pierre Lebeau), Best Actress (Karine Vanasse), Cinematography, Art Direction, Musical Score and Sound. An earlier film, A Man and His Sin (Un homme et son péché), based on the same novel, was released in 1949; the same novel was also the basis for the long-running dramatic television series Les Belles histoires des pays d'en haut.
