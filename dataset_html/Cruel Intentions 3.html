Cruel Intentions 3

Year: 2004

Genre: drama film

Country: United States

Plot: Cruel Intentions 3 is a 2004 American teen drama film directed by Scott Ziehl and released direct-to-video in 2004. It is the third film in the Cruel Intentions franchise. Despite its name, the film has almost no relation to the previous films in the series, except for the shared themes and the lead character in this film, Cassidy Merteuil, who is a cousin of one of the characters from the first film, Kathryn Merteuil.
