The Serpent's Kiss

Year: 1997

Genre: drama film

Country: France

Plot: The Serpent's Kiss is a 1997 British romantic drama film directed by Philippe Rousselot. Set in late-17th-century England, it tells the story of a Dutch garden architect, Meneer Chrome (Ewan McGregor), who has been hired by a wealthy merchant (Pete Postlethwaite) to create an extravagant garden. The film also stars Greta Scacchi and Richard E. Grant. The film was entered into the 1997 Cannes Film Festival. To date, it is the only feature directed by Rousselot, who usually works as cinematographer.
