When the Grapevines Bloom on the Danube

Year: 1965

Genre: romantic comedy

Country: Austria

Plot: When the Grapevines Bloom on the Danube is a 1965 German-Austrian comedy film of the heimatfilm genre directed by Géza von Cziffra and starring Hansjörg Felmy, Ingeborg Schöner and Gaston Brocksieper. The cinematographer was Heinz Pehlke. Its German title is An der Donau, wenn der Wein blüht.
