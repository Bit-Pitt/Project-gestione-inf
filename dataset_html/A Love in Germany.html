A Love in Germany

Year: 1983

Genre: drama film

Country: France

Plot: Eine Liebe in Deutschland (A Love in Germany) is a 1983 feature film directed by Andrzej Wajda. The film is based on the novel by Rolf Hochhuth about a woman who commits adultery with a prisoner of war while her husband serves as a soldier during World War II. A Love in Germany, featuring many popular German actors, was produced by German film producer Artur Brauner and German broadcaster ZDF.
