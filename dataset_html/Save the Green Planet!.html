Save the Green Planet!

Year: 2003

Genre: comedy film

Country: South Korea

Plot: Save the Green Planet! (Korean title: 지구를 지켜라!, Jigureul Jikyeora!) is a South Korean science fiction comedy film written and directed by Jang Joon-hwan, released on 4 April 2003. The basic story begins when the main character, Lee Byeong-gu, kidnaps another man, convinced that the latter is an alien. An English-language remake, titled Bugonia, will be released on November 7, 2025.
