Tell Me a Riddle

Year: 1980

Genre: drama film

Country: United States

Plot: Tell Me a Riddle is a collection of short fiction by Tillie Olsen first published by J. B. Lippincott & Co. in 1961. The volume is composed of three short stories and a novella, the title piece “Tell Me a Riddle.” “Tell Me a Riddle” was awarded the O. Henry Award in 1961 for best American short story. The works in the collection continue to be the most highly acclaimed of Olsen's literary oeuvre.
