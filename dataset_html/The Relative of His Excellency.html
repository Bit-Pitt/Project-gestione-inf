The Relative of His Excellency

Year: 1941

Genre: comedy film

Country: Hungary

Plot: 'The Relative of His Excellency' (Hungarian:A kegyelmes úr rokona) is a 1941 Hungarian comedy film directed by Félix Podmaniczky and starring László Szilassy, Erzsi Simor and Artúr Somlay. It was based on a novel by Zoltán Szitnyai. The production manager was Ernő Gottesmann.
