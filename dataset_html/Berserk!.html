Berserk!

Year: 1967

Genre: slasher film

Country: United Kingdom

Plot: Berserk! is a 1967 British horror-thriller film directed by Jim O'Connolly and starring Joan Crawford, Ty Hardin, Diana Dors and Judy Geeson. The screenplay was written by Herman Cohen and Aben Kandel. Berserk! marks Crawford's penultimate feature-film appearance.
