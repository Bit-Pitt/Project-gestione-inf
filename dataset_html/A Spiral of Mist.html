A Spiral of Mist

Year: 1977

Genre: drama film

Country: Italy

Plot: A Spiral of Mist (Italian: Una spirale di nebbia, French: Caresses bourgeoises, also known as Une spirale de brume) is a 1977 Italian-French thriller-drama film directed by Eriprando Visconti. It is based on the novel with the same name written by Michele Prisco.
