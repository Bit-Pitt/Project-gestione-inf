Philadelphia Experiment II

Year: 1993

Genre: drama film

Country: United States

Plot: Philadelphia Experiment II (also known as Philadelphia Experiment 2) is a 1993 science fiction film depicting a hypothetical Nazi-dominated America. It is the sequel to The Philadelphia Experiment (1984), with an entirely new cast and only two characters returning. It stars Brad Johnson as David Herdeg (the hero from the first film) and Gerrit Graham in a dual role as both the main villain and his father.
