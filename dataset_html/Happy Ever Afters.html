Happy Ever Afters

Year: 2009

Genre: romantic comedy

Country: Ireland

Plot: Happy Ever Afters is an Irish film written and directed by Stephen Burke and starring Sally Hawkins and Tom Riley. The film was first shown at the Pusan International Film Festival in South Korea on 10 October and released on 21 October 2009 in France. It tells the story of two weddings that collide when both receptions are held at one hotel. Happy Ever Afters is produced by Buena Vista International, Newgrange Pictures Ltd, Irish Film Board, Filmstiftung NRW and Films Distributions and distributed in the United Kingdom and Ireland by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures.
