A Lot Like Love

Year: 2005

Genre: drama film

Country: United States

Plot: A Lot like Love is a 2005 American romantic comedy-drama film directed by Nigel Cole and starring Ashton Kutcher and Amanda Peet. The screenplay by Colin Patrick Lynch focuses on two individuals whose relationship slowly evolves from lust to friendship to romance over the course of seven years. The film was released in the United States on April 22, 2005, recovered its production budget and received mixed reviews from critics.
