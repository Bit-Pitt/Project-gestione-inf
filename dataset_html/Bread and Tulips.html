Bread and Tulips

Year: 2000

Genre: romantic comedy

Country: Italy

Plot: Bread and Tulips (Italian: Pane e Tulipani) is a 2000 romance comedy film directed by Italian film director Silvio Soldini. The movie stars Licia Maglietta and Bruno Ganz as Rosalba Barletta and Fernando Girasole. The film was an official selection at numerous film festivals, including the Cannes Film Festival and the Toronto International Film Festival.
