Le Grand Chef

Year: 2007

Genre: drama film

Country: South Korea

Plot: Le Grand Chef (Korean: 식객; Hanja: 食客; RR: Sikgaek) is a 2007 South Korean comedy-drama film starring Kim Kang-woo, Im Won-hee and Lee Ha-na. Produced by ShowEast and distributed by CJ Entertainment, it was released on November 1, 2007 with the length of 114 minutes. Based on the popular manhwa Sikgaek by Huh Young-man, the film tells the story of two chefs competing for the title of heir to the last Royal Chef of the Joseon Dynasty.
