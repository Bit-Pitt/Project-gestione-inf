A Trip Without a Load

Year: 1962

Genre: drama film

Country: Soviet Union

Plot: A Trip Without a Load (Russian: Порожний рейс, romanized: Porozhniy reys) is a 1962 Soviet drama film directed by Vladimir Vengerov. It was entered into the 3rd Moscow International Film Festival at the last moment (instead of the film We, Two of Men) and won a Silver Prize.
