Tante Frieda – Neue Lausbubengeschichten

Year: 1965

Genre: comedy film

Country: Germany

Plot: Aunt Frieda (German: Tante Frieda – Neue Lausbubengeschichten) is a 1965 West German historical comedy film directed by Werner Jacobs and starring Elisabeth Flickenschildt, Hans Kraus and Gustav Knuth. It is based on the 1907 novel Tante Frieda by Ludwig Thoma. The film's sets were designed by the art director Wolf Englert.
