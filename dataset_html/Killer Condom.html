Killer Condom

Year: 1996

Genre: comedy film

Country: Switzerland

Plot: Killer Condom (German: Kondom des Grauens, lit. 'Condom of Horror') is a 1996 action comedy horror film directed by Martin Walz from a screenplay he co-wrote with Ralf König, based on the comic books Kondom des Grauens and Bis auf die Knochen (Down to the Bones) by König. It was distributed in the United States and Canada by Troma Entertainment, which promoted the film at the Cannes Film Festival with the help of "a six-foot-long fanged condom".
