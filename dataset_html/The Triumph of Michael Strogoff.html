The Triumph of Michael Strogoff

Year: 1961

Genre: film based on a novel

Country: Italy

Plot: The Triumph of Michael Strogoff (French: Le triomphe de Michel Strogoff) is a 1961 French-Italian historical adventure film directed by Viktor Tourjansky and starring Curd Jürgens, Capucine and Claude Titre. It is inspired by the 1876 novel Michael Strogoff by Jules Verne. Jürgens had previously played the role in the 1956 film Michel Strogoff. The film is presented as a sequel to Michel Strogoff, a 1926 film by the same director.
