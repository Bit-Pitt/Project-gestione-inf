The Shuttered Room

Year: 1967

Genre: horror film

Country: United Kingdom

Plot: The Shuttered Room (also known as Blood Island) is a 1967 British horror film directed by David Greene, and starring Gig Young and Carol Lynley. It was written by D. B. Ledrov and Nathaniel Tanchuck based on the 1959 short story of the same name by August Derleth, published as a so-called "posthumous collaboration" with H. P. Lovecraft. A couple move into a house with dark secrets.
