Zweiohrküken

Year: 2009

Genre: romantic comedy

Country: Germany

Plot: Rabbit Without Ears 2 (German title: Zweiohrküken, lit. "Two-Eared Chick") is a 2009 German romantic comedy film, written, produced and directed by Til Schweiger. A sequel to 2007's Rabbit Without Ears (Keinohrhasen), it was co-written by Anika Decker and Schweiger, starring Schweiger and Nora Tschirner as former yellow press reporter Ludo and his girlfriend Anna. Produced by Barefoot Films and Warner Bros. Germany, the film premiered in theaters across Germany and Austria on 3 December 2009.
