Thee Nagar

Year: 2007

Genre: action film

Country: India

Plot: Thee Nagar is a 2007 Indian Tamil-language action film directed by Thirumalai. It starred Karan and Udhayathara in lead roles, while Shanmugarajan, Scissor Manohar, Kadhal Sukumar, and Meera Krishnan play supporting roles. The soundtrack was composed by Jassie Gift with cinematography by Suresh Devan. The film was released on 27 July 2007.
