The Mexican

Year: 2001

Genre: romantic comedy

Country: United States

Plot: The Mexican is a 2001 American romantic crime comedy film directed by Gore Verbinski. The film stars Julia Roberts and Brad Pitt, with James Gandolfini, Bob Balaban, J. K. Simmons, and Gene Hackman in supporting roles. It tells the story of a small-time criminal (Pitt) who is sent on a mission to retrieve an antique pistol in Mexico, while his girlfriend (Roberts), frustrated with their troubled relationship, finds herself entangled in a series of unexpected events. The Mexican was released in the United States on March 2, 2001, to mixed reviews from critics, and grossed $147.8 million.
