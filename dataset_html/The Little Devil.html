The Little Devil

Year: 1988

Genre: comedy film

Country: Italy

Plot: The Little Devil (original Italian name Il piccolo diavolo) is a 1988 Italian surreal comedy film directed by and starring Roberto Benigni, also starring Walter Matthau, Stefania Sandrelli, Nicoletta Braschi and John Lurie. In Australia and some European countries, an English-language version of the film, with local subtitles, has been screened and circulated on VHS. In the English version, the voices of the main actors have been dubbed by themselves; some scenes might have been filmed in both languages.
