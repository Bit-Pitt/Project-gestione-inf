Never Forever

Year: 2007

Genre: drama film

Country: United States

Plot: Never Forever (Korean: 두번째 사랑; lit. Second Love) is a 2007 romantic drama film written and directed by Gina Kim, and starring Vera Farmiga. The film was critically acclaimed when it was first screened at the 2007 Sundance Film Festival, and won the Jury Prize at the Deauville American Film Festival.
