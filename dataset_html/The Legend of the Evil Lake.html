The Legend of the Evil Lake

Year: 2003

Genre: fantasy film

Country: South Korea

Plot: The Legend of the Evil Lake (Korean: 천년호; RR: Cheonnyeonho) is a 2003 South Korean fantasy/horror film directed by Lee Kwang-hoon. It is a remake of Shin Sang-ok's 1969 film A Thousand Year-Old Fox. The film was not particularly well received and was seen by 101,478 people at Seoul theaters.
