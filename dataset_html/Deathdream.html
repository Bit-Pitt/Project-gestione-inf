Deathdream

Year: 1974

Genre: drama film

Country: Canada

Plot: Deathdream (also known as Dead of Night or The Night Andy Came Home) is a 1974 horror film directed by Bob Clark and written by Alan Ormsby, and starring Richard Backus, John Marley, and Lynn Carlin. Filmed in Brooksville, Florida, it was inspired by the W. W. Jacobs short story "The Monkey's Paw".
