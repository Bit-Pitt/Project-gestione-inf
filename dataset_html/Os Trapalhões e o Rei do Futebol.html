Os Trapalhões e o Rei do Futebol

Year: 1986

Genre: comedy film

Country: Brazil

Plot: Os Trapalhões e o Rei do Futebol (English: The Tramps and the King of Football) is a Brazilian comedy film released in 1986, directed by Carlos Manga with the humoristic group Os Trapalhões and the former player Pelé. It was watched by about 3.6 million people in 1986.
