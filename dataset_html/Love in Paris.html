Love in Paris

Year: 1997

Genre: drama film

Country: United States

Plot: Love in Paris (released as Another 9½ Weeks in the United States) is a 1997 American erotic romantic drama film. It is a sequel to the 1986 film 9½ Weeks. Mickey Rourke reprises his role as John Gray from the original and Angie Everhart co-stars. The film, directed by Anne Goursaud was released directly to video in the United States, receiving poor reviews.
