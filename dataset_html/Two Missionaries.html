Two Missionaries

Year: 1974

Genre: comedy film

Country: Italy

Plot: Two Missionaries (Italian: Porgi l'altra guancia; Turn the Other Cheek) is a 1974 French/ Italian international co-production adventure comedy film starring the film duo of Terence Hill and Bud Spencer. The film is set in Venezuela. The film depicts two Roman Catholic missionaries who decide to raise parrots in their mission, and to defend the poor. The Bishop of Maracaibo considers the two misfits to be black sheep.
