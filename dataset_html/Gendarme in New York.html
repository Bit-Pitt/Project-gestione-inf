Gendarme in New York

Year: 1965

Genre: comedy film

Country: Italy

Plot: Gendarme in New York (French: Le Gendarme à New York) is a 1965 sequel to the French comedy film The Troops of St. Tropez. It stars Louis de Funès as the gendarme along with Michel Galabru, Christian Marin, Guy Grosso and Michel Modo.
