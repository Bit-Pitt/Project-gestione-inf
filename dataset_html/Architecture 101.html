Architecture 101

Year: 2012

Genre: drama film

Country: South Korea

Plot: Architecture 101 (Korean: 건축학개론; lit. Introduction to Architecture) is a 2012 South Korean romance film written and directed by Lee Yong-ju. The film tells the story of two students who meet in an introductory architecture class and fall in love. Fifteen years later, the girl tracks down her first love to seek his help in building her dream house.
