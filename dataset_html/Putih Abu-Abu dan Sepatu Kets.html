Putih Abu-Abu dan Sepatu Kets

Year: 2009

Genre: drama film

Country: Indonesia

Plot: Putih Abu-Abu dan Sepatu Kets is an Indonesian drama film released on October 29, 2009, directed by Nayato Fio Nuala and produced by Firman Bintang. It starring by Arumi Bachsin, Adipati Koesmadji, Michella Putri, Rendy Septino, Rana Audi Marissa, Fildha Elishandi, and Steven William.
