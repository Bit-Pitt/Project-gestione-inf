Dead or Alive 2: Birds

Year: 2000

Genre: action film

Country: Japan

Plot: Dead or Alive 2: Birds (DEAD OR ALIVE 2: 逃亡者, Deddo oa araibu 2: Toubousha, Lit. "Dead or Alive 2: Fugitives") is a 2000 Japanese film directed by Takashi Miike. Dead or Alive 2: Birds is unrelated to Dead or Alive (1999) or Dead or Alive: Final (2002) except that all three films have Show Aikawa and Riki Takeuchi in them, and they are all directed by Takashi Miike.
