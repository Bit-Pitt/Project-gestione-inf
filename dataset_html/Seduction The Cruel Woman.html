Seduction: The Cruel Woman

Year: 1985

Genre: drama film

Country: Germany

Plot: Seduction: The Cruel Woman (Verführung: Die grausame Frau) is a 1985 West German film, directed by Elfi Mikesch and Monika Treut, who both also wrote the screenplay. Wanda is played by Mechthild Großmann. The film was inspired by Leopold von Sacher-Masoch's Venus in Furs.
