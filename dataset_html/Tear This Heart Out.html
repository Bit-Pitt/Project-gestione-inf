Tear This Heart Out

Year: 2008

Genre: drama film

Country: Mexico

Plot: Tear This Heart Out (Spanish: Arráncame la vida) is a 2008 Mexican romantic historical drama film directed by Roberto Sneider based on the novel of the same name by Ángeles Mastretta. It was Mexico's official submission for the 2009 Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film, make the shortlist, but did not nominated.
