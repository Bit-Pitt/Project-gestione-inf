Who Do I Gotta Kill?

Year: 1994

Genre: comedy film

Country: United States

Plot: Who Do I Gotta Kill? (also known as Me & the Mob) is a 1994 comedy film directed and co-written by Frank Rainone with James Lorinz and Rocco Simonelli also writing. The film stars Sandra Bullock, John Costelloe, Steve Buscemi, and James Lorinz.
