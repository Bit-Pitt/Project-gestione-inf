The File of the Golden Goose

Year: 1969

Genre: drama film

Country: United Kingdom

Plot: The File of the Golden Goose is a 1969 British neo noir thriller film directed by Sam Wanamaker and starring Yul Brynner, Charles Gray and Edward Woodward. Its plot involves an American detective being sent to Britain to track down a major international criminal. It is a reworking of the 1947 American film noir T-Men, directed by Anthony Mann.
