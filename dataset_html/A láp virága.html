A láp virága

Year: 1943

Genre: drama film

Country: Hungary

Plot: The Marsh Flower (Hungarian: A láp virága) is a 1943 Hungarian drama film directed by Dezsõ Ákos Hamza and starring Pál Jávor, Alice Fényes and Nusi Somogyi. The film's sets were designed by the art directors István Básthy and Sándor Iliszi.
