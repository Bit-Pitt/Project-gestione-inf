2009: Lost Memories

Year: 2002

Genre: drama film

Country: Japan

Plot: 2009: Lost Memories (Korean: 2009 로스트 메모리즈) is a 2002 South Korean science fiction action film directed by Lee Si-myung, adapted from the 1987 novel Looking for an Epitaph by Bok Geo-il. The film takes place in an alternate 2009, where the Korean Peninsula is still part of Imperial Japan due to a time-travel incident in 1909. It was distributed by CJ Entertainment and was released on February 1, 2002.
