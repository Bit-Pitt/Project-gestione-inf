Surfer, Dude

Year: 2008

Genre: comedy film

Country: United States

Plot: Surfer, Dude is a 2008 American comedy film directed by S.R. Bindler and starring Matthew McConaughey. Woody Harrelson claims the film is the most "non-work" he has ever done. McConaughey and Harrelson, who previously appeared together in EDtv, later co-starred in HBO's True Detective.
