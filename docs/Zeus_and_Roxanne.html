Zeus and Roxanne

Year: 1997

Genre: children's film

Country: United States

Plot: Zeus and Roxanne is a 1997 American family adventure film directed by George T. Miller. It revolves around the friendship between the title characters Zeus and Roxanne, a dog and a dolphin, respectively, and the relationships between the human characters played by Steve Guttenberg, Kathleen Quinlan, and Miko Hughes. Produced by Rysher Entertainment, the film released theatrically in the United States on January 24, 1997, by Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer (MGM), grossing $7.2 million. The film received mixed reviews from critics.
