Agent 077: From the Orient with Fury

Year: 1966

Genre: action film

Country: Spain

Plot: Agent 077 From the Orient With Fury or Agent 077 Fury in the Orient or Agente 077 dall'oriente con furore or Fury on the Bosphorus is a 1965 Italian/Spanish/French international co-production action spy adventure film and the second of the Secret Agent 077 film series directed by Sergio Grieco. A Eurospy film, inspired by the James Bond series, it involves a scientist who has invented a disintegrator gun capable of mass destruction being kidnapped by a criminal gang for lethal usage.
