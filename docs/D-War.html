D-War

Year: 2007

Genre: drama film

Country: South Korea

Plot: D-War (Korean: 디워, released in North America as Dragon Wars: D-War) is a 2007 American-South Korean action-adventure fantasy film written and directed by Shim Hyung-rae, and starring Jason Behr, Amanda Brooks, Robert Forster, and Elizabeth Peña. At the time of its release, it was the highest-budgeted South Korean film of all time. The film grossed $75 million worldwide and received generally negative reviews.
