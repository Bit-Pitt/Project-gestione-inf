Incident at Map Grid 36-80

Year: 1982

Genre: thriller film

Country: Soviet Union

Plot: Incident at Map Grid 36-80 (Russian: Случай в квадрате 36-80, romanized: Sluchay v kvadrate 36-80) is a 1982 Soviet military action movie by Mikhail Tumanishvili. The total number of Soviet viewers was estimated at 33,100,000 people.
