I Could Never Be Your Woman

Year: 2007

Genre: romantic comedy

Country: United States

Plot: I Could Never Be Your Woman is a 2007 American romantic comedy film written and directed by Amy Heckerling and starring Michelle Pfeiffer and Paul Rudd, with Sarah Alexander, Stacey L. Dash, Jon Lovitz, Fred Willard, Saoirse Ronan, and Tracey Ullman. The film's title is a line from the 1997 song "Your Woman" by British artist White Town. It was released theatrically in several international markets beginning in Spain on May 11, 2007, while in the United States, it was released direct-to-DVD on February 12, 2008, receiving mixed to positive critic reviews.
