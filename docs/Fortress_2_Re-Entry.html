Fortress 2: Re-Entry

Year: 1999

Genre: action film

Country: United States

Plot: Fortress 2: Re-Entry is a 2000 science fiction action film directed by Geoff Murphy. It is the sequel to 1992's Fortress. In the film, the principal actor Christopher Lambert reprises his role as John Henry Brennick, still on the run from the MenTel Corporation. Lambert was the only original actor to reprise his role; Loryn Locklin, who played Karen B. Brennick from the first film, did not return for the sequel and she was played by Beth Toussaint.
