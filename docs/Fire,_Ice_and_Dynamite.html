Fire, Ice and Dynamite

Year: 1990

Genre: comedy film

Country: Germany

Plot: Fire, Ice and Dynamite (German original title Feuer, Eis und Dynamit) is a German feature-length sports film directed by Willy Bogner in 1990. It is a sequel to Fire and Ice. The screenplay was written by Tony Williamson, based on an original story by Willy Bogner. The film features cameos from Steffi Graf, Niki Lauda, Buzz Aldrin, Dennis Conner and Isaac Hayes.
