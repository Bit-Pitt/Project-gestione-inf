Sami swoi

Year: 1967

Genre: comedy film

Country: Poland

Plot: All Friends Here (Polish: Sami swoi, literally "only our own"), also known as Our Folks, is a 1967 film, and a first part of a Polish comedic trilogy of movies by Sylwester Chęciński. Its follow-ups are Nie ma mocnych (a Polish idiom meaning "no one can do") (1974), Kochaj albo rzuć ("Love or Leave") (1977), and Sami swoi. Początek ("All Friends Here. The Beginning") (2024).
