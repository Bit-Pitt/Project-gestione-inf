I Like You, I Like You Very Much

Year: 1994

Genre: pink film

Country: Japan

Plot: I Like You, I Like You Very Much (あなたが好きです、大好きです。, Anata-ga suki desu, dai suki desu) is a 1994 Japanese pink film written and directed by Hiroyuki Oki, depicting gay life in Japan. It won the Silver Prize at the 1994 Pink Grand Prix ceremony. Filmmaker/actor Hiroyuki Oki was also given a Best New Director award for the film.
