La mujer de mi hermano

Year: 2005

Genre: drama film

Country: Mexico

Plot: My Brother's Wife (Spanish: La mujer de mi hermano) is a 2005 Mexican film directed by Ricardo de Montreuil, based on the novel of the same name by the Peruvian writer, journalist and TV host Jaime Bayly. It starred Bárbara Mori, Manolo Cardona, Christian Meier, and Angélica Aragón.
