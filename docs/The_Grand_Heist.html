The Grand Heist

Year: 2012

Genre: comedy film

Country: South Korea

Plot: The Grand Heist (Korean: 바람과 함께 사라지다; lit. "Gone With the Wind") is a 2012 South Korean heist action comedy film about a gang of 11 thieves who try to steal ice blocks from the royal storage, Seobingo, during the last years of the Joseon era. It was released on August 8, 2012.
