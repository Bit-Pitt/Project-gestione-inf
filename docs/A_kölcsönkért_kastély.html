A kölcsönkért kastély

Year: 1937

Genre: comedy film

Country: Hungary

Plot: The Borrowed Castle (Hungarian: A kölcsönkért kastély) is a 1937 Hungarian romantic comedy film directed by Ladislao Vajda and starring Piroska Vaszary, Ida Turay and Imre Ráday. The film's sets were designed by the art director Márton Vincze.
