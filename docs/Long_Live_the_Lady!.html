Long Live the Lady!

Year: 1987

Genre: drama film

Country: Italy

Plot: Long Live the Lady! (Italian: Lunga vita alla signora!) is a 1987 Italian coming-of-age drama film written and directed by Ermanno Olmi. The film was entered into the main competition at the 44th edition of the Venice Film Festival, where it got the Silver Lion and the FIPRESCI Prize.
