Pouic-Pouic

Year: 1963

Genre: comedy film

Country: Italy

Plot: Pouic-Pouic is a French comedy film from 1963, directed by Jean Girault, written by Jean Girault and Jacques Vilfrid, starring Louis de Funès. The film is known under the titles: "Casamento a Propósito" (Portugal), "El pollo de mi mujer" (Spain), "Quietsch... quietsch... wer bohrt denn da nach Öl" (West Germany), "I 3 affari del signor Duval" (Italy).
