El bosque animado

Year: 1987

Genre: comedy film

Country: Spain

Plot: The Enchanted Forest (Spanish: El bosque animado) is a 1987 Spanish comedy-fantasy film directed by José Luis Cuerda. Rafael Azcona wrote the screenplay, based on the homonymous novel written by Wenceslao Fernández Flórez. The film has a large ensemble cast headed by Alfredo Landa. It was a critical success winning five Goya Awards including Best Film and Best Actor.
