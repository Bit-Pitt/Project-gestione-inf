Man, Next Natural Girl: 100 Nights in Yokohama

Year: 1999

Genre: horror film

Country: Japan

Plot: Man, Next Natural Girl: 100 Nights in Yokohama (天然少女萬NEXT　横浜百夜篇, Tennen shōjo Man next: Yokohama hyaku-ya hen, also known as N-Girls Vs Vampire and Man, Next Natural Girl: Perfect Box) is a 1999 two-part Japanese television series directed by Takashi Miike.
