Two Tickets to Broadway

Year: 1951

Genre: musical film

Country: United States

Plot: Two Tickets to Broadway is a 1951 American musical film directed by James V. Kern and starring Tony Martin, Janet Leigh, Gloria DeHaven and Ann Miller. It was filmed on the RKO Forty Acres backlot. It was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Sound Recording (John O. Aalberg). The film was choreographed by Busby Berkeley. The film recorded an estimated loss of $1,150,000.
