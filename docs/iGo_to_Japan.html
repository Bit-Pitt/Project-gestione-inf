iGo to Japan

Year: 2008

Genre: adventure film

Country: United States

Plot: iGo to Japan is a 2008 television film, serving as the fifth episode of the second season of Nickelodeon TV series iCarly. It premiered on November 8, 2008, on Nickelodeon, and November 21 on YTV. The television movie stars Miranda Cosgrove, Jennette McCurdy, Nathan Kress, Jerry Trainor, and Mary Scheer. The film was directed by Steve Hoefer. Production began in early 2008 and lasted 4 to 5 weeks.
