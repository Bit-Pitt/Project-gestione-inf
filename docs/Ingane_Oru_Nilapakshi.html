Ingane Oru Nilapakshi

Year: 2000

Genre: Unknown

Country: India

Plot: Ingane Oru Nilapakshi (transl. A moonlight bird like this) is a 2000 Indian Malayalam-language romantic drama film directed by Anil–Babu and starring Kunchako Boban, Sneha (credited as Manasi), Sangeetha (credited as Rasika) and Sujitha. The film was a box office failure. This film is recognized for being Sneha's debut film.
