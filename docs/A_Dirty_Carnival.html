A Dirty Carnival

Year: 2006

Genre: drama film

Country: South Korea

Plot: A Dirty Carnival (Korean: 비열한 거리; lit. The Mean Street) is a 2006 South Korean neo-noir action film directed by Yoo Ha. It is Yoo Ha's fourth feature film, his previous work being the 2004 drama Once Upon a Time in High School. The film was released on 15 June 2006.
