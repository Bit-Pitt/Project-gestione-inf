The Aimless Bullet

Year: 1961

Genre: drama film

Country: South Korea

Plot: Obaltan (Korean: 오발탄; also known as Aimless Bullet and Stray Bullet) is a 1961 South Korean tragedy film directed by Yu Hyun-mok. The plot is based on the novella of the same name by Yi Beomseon. It has often been called the best Korean movie ever made.
