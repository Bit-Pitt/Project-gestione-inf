3 Ninjas Knuckle Up

Year: 1995

Genre: action film

Country: United States

Plot: 3 Ninjas Knuckle Up is a 1995 American martial arts comedy film and a sequel to 3 Ninjas and 3 Ninjas Kick Back. The film was directed by Shin Sang-ok, using the pseudonym "Simon Sheen". The film was shot in 1992, the same year the first film was released, but was not released in the United States until 1995.
