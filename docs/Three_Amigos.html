It is the story of three American silent film stars who are mistaken for real heroes by the suffering people of a small Mexican village. The actors must find a way to live up to their reputation and stop a malevolent group of bandits. Orion Pictures released Three Amigos on December 12, 1986, and it earned $39.2 million against a budget of $25 million.
