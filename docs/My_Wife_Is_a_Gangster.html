My Wife Is a Gangster

Year: 2001

Genre: action film

Country: South Korea

Plot: My Wife Is a Gangster (Korean: 조폭 마누라) is a 2001 South Korean action romantic comedy film directed by Jo Jin-kyu and written by Kang Hyo-jin and Kim Moon-sung. A sequel titled My Wife Is a Gangster 2 was released in 2003, with a third film (My Wife Is a Gangster 3) released in 2006.
