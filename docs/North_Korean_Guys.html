North Korean Guys

Year: 2003

Genre: comedy film

Country: South Korea

Plot: North Korean Guys (Korean: 동해물과 백두산이; RR: Donghaemul-gwa Baekdusan-i; lit. Eastern Sea's Waters and Paektu Mountain's), also released as Lost in the South Mission: Going Home, is a 2003 South Korean comedy film directed by Ahn Jin-woo. The film depicts a pair of North Korean sailors who find themselves in South Korea and their subsequent attempts to return home.
