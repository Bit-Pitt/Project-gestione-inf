Post Grad

Year: 2009

Genre: romantic comedy

Country: United States

Plot: Post Grad is a 2009 American romantic comedy-drama film directed by Vicky Jenson (in her first solo directorial film) and starring Alexis Bledel, about a recent college graduate who moves back in with her family while she figures out what she wants to do next. Originally under the working titles of Ticket to Ride and then The Post-Grad Survival Guide, the film was released on August 21, 2009, to a critical and commercial failure.
