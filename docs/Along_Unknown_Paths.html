Along Unknown Paths

Year: 1982

Genre: cinematic fairy tale

Country: Soviet Union

Plot: Along Unknown Paths (Russian: Там, на неведомых дорожках..., romanized: Tam, na nevedomykh dorozhkakh) is a 1982 children's fantasy film directed by Mikhail Yuzovsky based on the novel Down by the Magic River by Eduard Uspensky.
