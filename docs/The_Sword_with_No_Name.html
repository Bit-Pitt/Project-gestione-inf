The Sword with No Name

Year: 2009

Genre: drama film

Country: South Korea

Plot: The Sword with No Name (Korean: 불꽃처럼 나비처럼; RR: Bulkkotcheoreom Nabicheoreom; lit. Like Fireworks, Like Butterflies) is a 2009 South Korean historical action drama film, starring Cho Seung-woo and Soo Ae. This film is based on a fictionalized account of Empress Myeongseong. The film sold 1,671,387 tickets nationwide.
