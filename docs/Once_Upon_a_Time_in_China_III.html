Once Upon a Time in China III

Year: 1993

Genre: action film

Country: Hong Kong

Plot: Once Upon a Time in China III is a 1993 Hong Kong martial arts film written, produced and directed by Tsui Hark. Jet Li stars as Chinese martial arts master and folk hero of Cantonese ethnicity, Wong Fei-hung. It is the third installment in the Once Upon a Time in China film series.
