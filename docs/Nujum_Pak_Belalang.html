Nujum Pak Belalang

Year: 1959

Genre: musical film

Country: Singapore

Plot: Nujum Pak Belalang (English: The Fortune-telling of Pak Belalang) is a 1959 Singaporean Malay-language black-and-white comedy film directed by and starring P. Ramlee. The film is styled as a fairytale and is loosely based on a Malay folk tale.
