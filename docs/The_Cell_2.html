The Cell 2

Year: 2009

Genre: horror film

Country: United States

Plot: The Cell 2 is a 2009 direct-to-video sequel to the 2000 film The Cell. Directed by Tim Iacofano and written by Lawerence Silverstein, Alex Barder and Rob Rinow, the movie is similar in themes as the original, about an investigator who must enter the mind of a serial killer.
