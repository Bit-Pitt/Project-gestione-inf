The Bells Have Gone to Rome

Year: 1958

Genre: drama film

Country: Hungary

Plot: The Bells Have Gone to Rome (Hungarian: A harangok Rómába mentek) is a 1959 Hungarian drama film directed by Miklós Jancsó and starring Miklós Gábor, Vilmos Mendelényi and Gabi Magda. It was Jancsó's first feature film having previously directed a number of short films.
