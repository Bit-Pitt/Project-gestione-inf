Five Senses of Eros

Year: 2009

Genre: romance film

Country: South Korea

Plot: Five Senses of Eros (Korean: 오감도; RR: Ogamdo) is a 2009 South Korean omnibus film with five short films depicting love and desire, but in different styles and genres. The shorts are: His Concern, directed by Daniel H. Byun; I'm Right Here (나, 여기 있어요; Na, Yeogi Isseoyo), directed by Hur Jin-ho; The 33rd Man (33번째 남자; 33beonjjae Namja), directed by Yoo Young-sik; In My End Is My Beginning (끝과 시작; Ggeutgwa Sijak), directed by Min Kyu-dong; and Believe in the Moment (순간을 믿어요; Sunganeul Mideoyo), directed by Oh Ki-hwan. The five directors are all graduates of the Korean Academy of Film Arts. The film received 438,501 admissions nationwide.
