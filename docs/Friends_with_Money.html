Friends with Money

Year: 2006

Genre: drama film

Country: United States

Plot: Friends with Money is a 2006 American comedy-drama film written and directed by Nicole Holofcener. It stars Jennifer Aniston, Joan Cusack, Catherine Keener, Frances McDormand, Jason Isaacs, Scott Caan, Simon McBurney, and Greg Germann, and follows the personal relationships of a group of friends. It premieresd at the 2006 Sundance Film Festival on January 19, 2006 and was released in the United States on April 7, 2006.
