5 Days of War

Year: 2011

Genre: drama film

Country: United States

Plot: 5 Days of War is a 2011 war film directed by Renny Harlin. The story is about the Russo-Georgian War over the Russian-backed breakaway autonomous republic of South Ossetia in Georgia, including the events leading up to the conflict. The film was released in Georgia as 5 Days of August, and in other countries as 5 Days of War and also City on Fire.
