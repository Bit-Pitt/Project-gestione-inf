Temptation of Wolves

Year: 2004

Genre: drama film

Country: South Korea

Plot: Temptation of Wolves (Korean: 늑대의 유혹; RR: Neukdaeui yuhok; also known as Romance of Their Own or True Romance) is a 2004 South Korean romantic drama film directed by Kim Tae-kyun, and based on a web novel of the same name written by Guiyeoni. Starring Jo Han-sun, Gang Dong-won and Lee Chung-ah, the film is about an average high school girl who moves from the countryside to Seoul after her father's death only to become involved in a love triangle with the two most handsome and popular guys in town. 2,189,453 admissions made it the 9th highest grossing Korean film of 2004.
