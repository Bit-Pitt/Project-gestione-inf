A 9-es kórterem

Year: 1955

Genre: romance film

Country: Hungary

Plot: Ward 9 (Hungarian: A 9-es kórterem) is a 1955 Hungarian drama film directed by Károly Makk and starring Marianne Krencsey, Tibor Molnár and Zoltán Makláry. It was shot at the Hunnia Studios in Budapest. The film's sets were designed by the art director Mátyás Varga.
