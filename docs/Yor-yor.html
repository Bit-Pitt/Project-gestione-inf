Yor-yor

Year: 1964

Genre: musical

Country: Soviet Union

Plot: Yor-yor or Gde ty, moya Zulfiya? (transliteration of the Russian title of the film meaning "Where are You, My Zulfiya?") (Uzbek: Yor-yor / Ёр-ёр; Russian: Где ты моя, Зульфия?) is a 1964 Uzbek comedy produced by Ali Hamroyev. The film is considered to be one of the best Uzbek comedies and has been dubbed the "national comedy."
