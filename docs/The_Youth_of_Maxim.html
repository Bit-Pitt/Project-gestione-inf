The Youth of Maxim

Year: 1935

Genre: drama film

Country: Soviet Union

Plot: The Youth of Maxim (Russian: Юность Максима) is a 1935 Soviet historical drama film directed by Grigori Kozintsev and Leonid Trauberg, the first part of trilogy about the life of a young factory worker named Maxim. It was followed in 1937 by The Return of Maxim.
