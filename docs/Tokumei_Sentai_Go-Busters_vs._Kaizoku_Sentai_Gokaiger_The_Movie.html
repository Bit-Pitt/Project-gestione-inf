Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters vs. Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger: The Movie

Year: 2013

Genre: crossover fiction

Country: Japan

Plot: Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters vs. Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger: The Movie (特命戦隊ゴーバスターズVS海賊戦隊ゴーカイジャー THE MOVIE, Tokumei Sentai Gōbasutāzu Tai Kaizoku Sentai Gōkaijā Za Mūbī) is an entry in the Super Sentai VS film series, which features the meeting of casts and characters of Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters and Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger. The film was released on January 19, 2013 and features the first appearance of the main cast of Zyuden Sentai Kyoryuger.
