Year: 2012

Country: Germany

Plot: "Your Place" is song by American country music artist Ashley Cooke. It was released on December 11, 2023 as the lead single from her second studio album Shot in the Dark. Cooke co-wrote the song with Jordan Minton and Mark Trussell and it was produced by Jimmy Robbins. Cooke was the recipient of iHeartRadio's On the Verge program. The song reached #2 on the Billboard Country Airplay chart on the week of June 29, 2024, behind "I Had Some Help" by Post Malone and Morgan Wallen.
