King Kong Appears in Edo

Year: 1938

Genre: fantasy film

Country: Japan

Plot: The King Kong That Appeared in Edo (江戸に現れたキングコング, Edo ni Arawareta Kingu Kongu), commonly erroneously translated as King Kong Appears in Edo, is a 1938 Japanese two-part silent jidaigeki film produced by Zenshō Cinema. It is now considered to be a lost film.
