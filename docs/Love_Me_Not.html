Love Me Not

Year: 2006

Genre: drama film

Country: South Korea

Plot: Love Me Not (Korean: 사랑따윈 필요없어, lit. "Love and Such Is Not Necessary") is a 2006 South Korean romance film directed by Lee Cheol-ha and starring Moon Geun-young and Kim Joo-hyuk. It is based on the 2002 TBS Japanese drama Forget Love (愛なんていらねえよ、夏, Ai nante Irane yo, Natsu), starring Ryōko Hirosue. The 2013 South Korean television series That Winter, the Wind Blows is also based on the same storyline.
