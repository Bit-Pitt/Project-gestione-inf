Twenty Girls and the Teachers

Year: 1971

Genre: comedy film

Country: Germany

Plot: Twenty Girls and the Teachers (German: Zwanzig Mädchen und die Pauker: Heute steht die Penne kopf) is a 1971 West German comedy film directed by Werner Jacobs and starring Mascha Gonska, Heidi Kabel, Rudolf Schündler and Fritz Tillmann.
