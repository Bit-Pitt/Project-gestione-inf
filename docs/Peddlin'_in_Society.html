Peddlin' in Society

Year: 1946

Genre: comedy film

Country: Italy

Plot: Peddlin' in Society or Down With Riches! (Italian: Abbasso la ricchezza!) is a 1946 Italian romance-drama film directed by Gennaro Righelli. it was a loose sequel to the 1945 film Down with Misery by the same director and also starring Magnani.
