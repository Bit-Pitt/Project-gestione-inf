The NeverEnding Story

Year: 1984

Genre: drama film

Country: Germany

Plot: The Neverending Story (German: Die unendliche Geschichte) is a fantasy novel by German writer Michael Ende, published in 1979. The first English translation, by Ralph Manheim, was published in 1983. It was later adapted into a film series and a television series, The NeverEnding Story: The Animated Adventures of Bastion Balthazar Bux.
