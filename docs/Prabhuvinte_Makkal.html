Prabhuvinte Makkal

Year: 2012

Genre: drama film

Country: India

Plot: Prabhuvinte Makkal is a 2012 Indian Malayalam-language drama film written and directed by Sajeevan Anthikad, and starring Vinay Forrt, Jijoy Rajagopal, Swasika, and Madhu. The film discusses the theme of atheism. It was debut film of Tovino Thomas.
