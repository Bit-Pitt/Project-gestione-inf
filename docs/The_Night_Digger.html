The Night Digger

Year: 1971

Genre: thriller film

Country: United Kingdom

Plot: The Road Builder (U.S. title: The Night Digger) is a 1971 British thriller film directed by Alastair Reid and starring Patricia Neal, Pamela Brown and Nicholas Clay. The screenplay was by Roald Dahl based on the novel Nest in a Fallen Tree by Joy Cowley, about two women who are visited by a suspicious handy man.
