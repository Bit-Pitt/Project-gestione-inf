Lovers Vanished

Year: 2010

Genre: romance film

Country: South Korea

Plot: Lovers Vanished (Korean: 폭풍전야; RR: Pokpungjoenya; lit. Before the Storm) is a 2010 South Korean film, directed by Cho Chang-ho as both a love story and a character drama. It stars Kim Nam-gil and Hwang Woo-seul-hye as two people who find love at the edge of life.
