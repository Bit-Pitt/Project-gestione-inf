Asiris Nuna

Year: 2006

Genre: fantasy film

Country: Russia

Plot: Asiris Nuna (Russian: Азирис Нуна) is a 2006 Russian children's science fiction film directed by Oleg Kompasov and produced by Vladimir Khrapunov, Aleksei Kuznetsov and Sergey Chernyakov. The film is based on a Yuli Burkin and Sergei Lukyanenko novel Today, mom!, the first part of Island Rus trilogy, adapted by Ramil Farzutdinov and Oleg Kompasov. Music by Dmitri Funtikov.
