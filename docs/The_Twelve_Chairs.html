The Twelve Chairs

Year: 1970

Genre: comedy film

Country: United States

Plot: The Twelve Chairs (Russian: Двенадцать стульев, romanized: Dvenadtsat stulyev) is a Russian classic satirical picaresque novel by the Soviet authors Ilf and Petrov, published in 1928. Its plot follows characters attempting to obtain jewelry hidden in a chair. A sequel was published in 1931. The novel has been adapted to other media, primarily film.
