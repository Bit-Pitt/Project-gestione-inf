Death Bell 2: Bloody Camp

Year: 2010

Genre: horror film

Country: South Korea

Plot: Death Bell 2: Bloody Camp (Korean: 고死 두번째 이야기 : 교생실습; RR: Gosa dubeonjjae iyagi : Gyosaengsilseup) is a 2010 South Korean slasher film. It was directed by Yoo Sun-dong and is a sequel to the 2008 film Death Bell. The story is unrelated to the previous film. The film was also pre-sold in Taiwan and Hong Kong for $230,000 at the 63rd Cannes International Film Festival Film Market.
