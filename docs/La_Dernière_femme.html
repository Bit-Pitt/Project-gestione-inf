La Dernière femme

Year: 1976

Genre: drama film

Country: Italy

Plot: The Last Woman (French: La Dernière femme, Italian: L'ultima donna, German: Die letzte Frau) is a 1976 French-Italian film directed by Marco Ferreri and starring Gérard Depardieu and Ornella Muti. Depardieu was nominated for best actor for his role in the film at the César ceremony in 1977.
