36 Views from the Pic Saint-Loup

Year: 2009

Genre: drama film

Country: Italy

Plot: Around a Small Mountain (French: 36 vues du pic Saint-Loup) is a 2009 French drama film directed by Jacques Rivette, which was his final film before his death on 29 January 2016, and starred Jane Birkin and Sergio Castellitto. It was screened in the main competition at the 66th Venice International Film Festival.
