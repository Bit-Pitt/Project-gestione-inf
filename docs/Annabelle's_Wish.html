Annabelle's Wish

Year: 1997

Genre: adventure film

Country: United States

Plot: Annabelle's Wish is a 1997 American direct-to-video animated Christmas film that revolves around a young calf who aspires to fly and become one of Santa Claus's reindeer. Narrated by American country singer Randy Travis, it also stars voice actress Kath Soucie as the voice of Annabelle. Ralph Edwards Films released the film to video on October 21, 1997, followed by a television broadcast later that year on Fox.
