Ghost Sweepers

Year: 2012

Genre: comedy horror

Country: South Korea

Plot: Ghost Sweepers (Korean: 점쟁이들; RR: Jeomjaengyideul; MR: Chŏmjaengidŭl; also known as The Fortune Tellers) is a 2012 South Korean comedy horror film about a group of shamans from across the nation who gather together for a grand exorcism at a small village notorious for dark spirits, as a young journalist gets sent to cover the story.
