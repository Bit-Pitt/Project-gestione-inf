In the Beginning There Was Light

Year: 2010

Genre: documentary film

Country: Austria

Plot: In The Beginning There Was Light is a documentary film by Austrian director P. A. Straubinger on the subject of inedia. Straubinger visits several people who supposedly nourish themselves with "light" and tries to find possible explanations on how inedia might work. Straubinger researched inedia for ten years.
