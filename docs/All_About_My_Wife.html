All About My Wife

Year: 2012

Genre: romantic comedy

Country: South Korea

Plot: All About My Wife (Korean: 내 아내의 모든 것) is a 2012 South Korean romantic comedy film directed by Min Kyu-dong, about a timid husband who hires a professional Casanova to seduce his seemingly perfect but fearsome wife, hoping this will make her divorce him. Starring Im Soo-jung, Lee Sun-kyun and Ryu Seung-ryong, the movie was released in theaters on May 17, 2012. It is a remake of the Argentine film Un novio para mi mujer ("A Boyfriend for My Wife").
